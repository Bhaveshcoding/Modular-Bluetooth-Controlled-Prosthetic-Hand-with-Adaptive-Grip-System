Modular Bluetooth-Controlled Prosthetic Hand with Adaptive Grip System

MECHANICAL-
	- 5 Fingers
		-3 segment pull
	- String based pulling
		-nylon rope
		-fishing rope
	-Rubber Band return


ELECTRONICS-
	-Arduino UNO R3
	-5 servo motors
		-1servo/finger
	-Bluetooth Control


CONTROLS-
	-Predefined grip modes(using Blue tooth)
		-OPEN
		-CLOSE
		-SEMIOPEN
		-INDIVIDUAL FINGER MOVEMENT


CONTROL METHODS-
	-Bluetooth

DESIGN-
	-3D printed parts
